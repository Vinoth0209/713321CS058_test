Our Story


More than a decade ago, Golden State Property Management emerged as the quintessential sanctuary for property owners and tenants in the South Bay. Our comprehensive approach to property management has established us as the preeminent firm in the region, earning the trust of our clientele. It brings us great pride to note that former tenants turned landlords consistently return to us for their property management requirements.

As a distinguished member of the Silicon Valley Association of Realtors, the National Association of Realtors, and a Past President of the local National Association of Residential Property Managers Chapter (NARPM), along with affiliations with the Tri-County Apartment Association, Rental Housing Network, Apartment Owners Association, and the California Apartment Association, Golden State Property Management remains abreast of evolving local and state laws impacting investment real estate.

We eagerly anticipate the opportunity to support you with your complete property management needs. Feel free to reach out to us at any time for inquiries about our services or general questions concerning investment properties.



Our Company


At our core, we prioritize the significance of treating property owners, tenants, and the homes our renters occupy with dignity and respect. Our commitment revolves around stewardship and providing compassionate care to tenants. Nestled in the heart of the Silicon Valley, we harness cutting-edge technology to address the diverse needs of investors, properties, and tenants.

Innovation is ingrained in our approach. Leveraging technology, we ensure prompt services for both clients and tenants. Owners can access details about their investment properties online, while maintenance requests from tenants are seamlessly submitted to our maintenance department through an online platform. Additionally, online channels facilitate hassle-free rental payments.
We believe in unification. Through offering comprehensive services encompassing marketing, collections, bill payment, and maintenance, we foster a unified guardianship for property owners and residents alike.

Our dedication sets us apart as the South Bay’s premier destination for property owners and renters. Tenants often transition into landlords because we uphold our commitment to enhancing investors’ reputations in the real estate marketplace.

If you’re seeking a place to call home, explore our vacancies section. For those ready to delve into our complete property management services in San Mateo, San Jose, Fremont, or surrounding areas, feel free to contact us for further discussion. Welcome to a comprehensive and forward-thinking property management experience!
